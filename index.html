Factory Visit & Manufacturing

An overview of how chocolate is made.

1
Bean Sourcing

High-quality cocoa beans are ethically sourced from certified farms and checked for quality.

2
Roasting & Grinding

Beans are roasted and ground to release rich cocoa flavor.

3
Mixing & Conching

Milk, sugar and cocoa are gently mixed and conched to achieve a smooth texture.

4
Tempering & Molding

Chocolate is tempered for shine and snap, then poured into molds for final shape.

5
Packing & Quality

Each pack is inspected, wrapped and prepared for distribution with safety standards.
